About

CV

I'm currently a fourth-year Ph.D. student at the University of Hong Kong, advised by Prof. Xiaojuan Qi. Before that, I obtained my B.Eng. degree (Ranked 3/153, GPA 3.95/4.0 or 91.25/100) in Automation at Zhejiang University (2021) supervised by Prof. Yong Liu, and I was also at Chu Kochen Honors College's mixed class.

My research interest lies in the wild areas of artificial intelligence and computer vision, with a focus on data-centric/efficient learning (e.g. synthetic/generative data, mixing multi-modal data, and semi-supervised learning) for computer vision tasks. During my Ph.D. study, I have been fortunate to intern at ByteDance AI Lab, advised by Song Bai.

I'm currently seeking full-time opportunities starting in Summer 2025, please feel free to reach out if you have any openings!
