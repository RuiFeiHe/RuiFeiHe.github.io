Research Statement

I'm currently a 3rd-year undergraduate student at ZJU's Control Science and Engineering Department, and a member of the Robotics and Machine Vision Laboratory. I'm also at Chu Kochen Honors College's mixed class. My research interest lies in the areas of artificial intelligence and computer vision, with a focus on optical flow estimation, scene understanding, and video processing.

My academic performance has achieved excellent results throughout my undergraduate years. My major ranking has improved from 4/111(freshmen) to 1/153(sophomore), with an average GPA of 3.95/4.0 currently.

I'm highly motivated in conducting more in-depth research to figure out different issues in the field of computer vision. One of my research goals is to prepare myself to be a qualified PhD candidate, and my long-term goal is to dedicate myself into computer vision and being a researcher in this field.
